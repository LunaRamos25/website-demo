About Us

CAPT (Captivate) is a creative studio specializing in video editing, graphic design, social media marketing, and web design. Founded in 2020, we have evolved from a small freelance initiative into a dedicated team serving both local businesses and international brands.

Our approach goes beyond aesthetics — we craft compelling narratives, design purposeful visuals, and develop strategies that drive measurable growth. By combining creativity with technical expertise, we help our clients strengthen their digital presence and connect authentically with their audiences.

Over the years, CAPT has partnered with organizations across industries, delivering content and campaigns that inspire, engage, and convert. Our mission is to captivate attention, elevate brands, and deliver results that matter.

Our Services

Helping you grow through creative content, smart design, and technical expertise.

Video Editing

Story-driven edits tailored for web and social. Hooks, pacing, subtitles, color and sound design, plus platform-native formats to maximize retention.

Graphic Design

Visual systems that scale—logos, brand kits, social templates, pitch decks, and campaign assets for both print and digital, built for consistency and clarity.

Facebook Ads

Full-funnel campaigns with creative testing, precise targeting, pixel/UTM setup, and reporting focused on conversions and ROAS.

UGC Contents

Authentic, creator-led videos that feel native to the feed. We handle sourcing, briefs, scripts, and edits optimized for vertical viewing.

Web Development

Fast, responsive sites with clean UX, SEO-friendly structure, analytics, and simple content management—built for performance and scale.

Game Development

From prototype to launch—core loops, UI/UX, assets, polish, and publishing support for engaging player experiences.
